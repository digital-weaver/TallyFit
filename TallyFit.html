Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Tap the Profile tab and select "Create Account". Enter your email and follow the verification steps. Your workouts sync across devices when signed in.

How does Tally (AI Assistant) work?

Tally is your AI workout partner that helps create personalized routines and exercises. Share your goals and limitations to get tailored suggestions. Always verify for accuracy and safety.

How do I manage my subscription?

You can manage your subscription through your Apple ID settings:

  • Open iPhone Settings
  • Tap your Apple ID
  • Select Subscriptions
  • Find TallyFit to manage or cancel

How do I sync my workouts across devices?

Once signed in, your workouts sync automatically when online. You can still create workouts offline; they’ll sync when you reconnect.

What if Tally gives me inappropriate or incorrect suggestions?

Review all suggestions for personal suitability. Start with lower intensity, stop if something feels wrong, and report unsafe suggestions to support.

How do I export or delete my data?

Email us your request from your account email. We aim to process within 48 hours, though delays may occur.
