Privacy Policy

TallyFit does not collect, store, or share any personal data. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

Effective Date: 09/30/2024

Information Collection and Use

TallyFit does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All information you enter into the app is stored locally on your device.

Data Sharing

When you choose to share a workout routine, only the information you've entered about that routine is shared. This sharing is initiated by you, and the data is only sent to the recipient you choose.

Data Security

As all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your information is protected by your device's built-in security features.
