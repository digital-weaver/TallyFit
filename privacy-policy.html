Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 27, 2024

1. Introduction

TallyFit respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Contact Information: Email address for account creation and communication
  • Account Identifiers: User ID and Device ID for app functionality
  • Technical Data: Crash reports and diagnostic information
  • Workout Data: Your custom workouts, exercise preferences, and routines
  • AI Interaction Data: Your conversations with Tally, our AI assistant

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for:

  • Providing and improving our services
  • Syncing your workouts across devices
  • Training and improving our AI assistant
  • Analyzing app performance and fixing issues
  • Communicating important updates

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use Firebase for authentication and data storage, ensuring your information is protected according to Google's security standards.

5. AI Features and Data Processing

Our AI assistant, Tally, processes your workout preferences and conversations to provide personalized recommendations. This data is:

  • Used to improve AI responses and workout suggestions
  • Processed in accordance with privacy best practices
  • Never shared with third parties for marketing purposes

6. Data Not Linked to You

The following data is collected but not linked to your identity:

  • Diagnostic information
  • App usage statistics
  • Crash reports

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can request data deletion by contacting us.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request data deletion
  • Export your data

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app.
