Privacy Policy

TallyFit does not collect, store, or share any personal data. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

Effective Date: 09/30/2024

Information Collection and Use

TallyFit does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All information you enter into the app, such as custom exercises and routines, is stored locally on your device.

Data Sharing

When you choose to share a workout routine, only the information you've entered about that routine (such as exercise names and custom routine names) is shared. This sharing is initiated by you, and the data is only sent to the recipient you choose.

Disclaimer: Please do not input any sensitive or personal information (e.g., passwords, personal identification numbers) in the workout routine fields, as this information will be shared as part of the routine if shared with others.

Third-Party Services

TallyFit does not use any third-party services that collect data.

Data Security

As all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your information is protected by your device's built-in security features.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
